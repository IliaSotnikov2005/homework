The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
The dog is brown and lazy. Foxes are quick and brown.
The quick fox jumps over the lazy dog, but the lazy dog
doesn't move. The brown fox looks back at the lazy dog.
The dog watches the fox. Foxes and dogs are both mammals.
They have fur and tails. The quick fox has a bushy tail.
The lazy dog's tail is wagging. Foxes and dogs are friends
in some stories. They play together in the forest. The quick
fox and the lazy dog are happy together.
